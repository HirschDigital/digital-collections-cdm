Latin American Art and LatinX Art

About this Collection

With the establishment of the Latin American aArt department in 2001, the Hirsch Library began to develop related collections that reflect the significant breadth and depth of Latin American and LatinX creative practices. This resulted in rich research collections comprised ofcomprising illustrated periodicals, biennial publications, manifestos, conceptual works, and poster productions from across Latin America and the diaspora. Noteworthy among our resources is printed matter ranging from Brazilian Constructivism and to Mexican Modernism to Chicano graphics and to Uruguayan Universalism.
