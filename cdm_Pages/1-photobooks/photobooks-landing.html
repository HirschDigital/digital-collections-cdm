Photobooks in the Libraries and Archives

About this Collection

Over the past few decades the Hirsch Library has made a significant commitment to acquiring photographically illustrated publications as a distinct genre of artistic object. In the process, we have developed a robust photobook collection. Central to these efforts was the 2012 acquisition of the unparalleled Manfred Heiting Book Collection, an internationally recognized photography book collection. The Hirsch and Heiting collections provide the rare opportunity to explore the world class photography collection housed here at the MFAH alongside an equally strong photography book collection, enabling patrons to discover more about the parallel, and often conjoined, histories of photography and the photographically illustrated book.
