Jay I. Kislak Collection of the Early Americas, Exploration and Navigation

From the Cuban Heritage Collection

Lydia Cabrera Papers

Features letters, photographs, research notes, and manuscripts from the papers of Lydia Cabrera, one of the 20th century's leading writers on Cuban folklore and an internationally known chronicler Afro-Cuban religion and culture.

From Special Collections

Diaspora Vibe Cultural Arts Incubator Records

Artists' files from artists featured in the Diaspora Vibe Cultural Arts Incubator, created to promote the diverse talents of emerging artists from the Caribbean and the Latin American diaspora.

From the University Archives

University of Miami University Communications Collection

Photographs documenting the University of Miami featuring student life, athletics, campus events, faculty, and administration from the 1980s to the early 2000s.

Erwin G. Harris Archive

Correspondence, legal documents, advertising materials, graphic design proofs, and more pertaining to Erwin G. Harris and his advertising enterprises.

Sea Frontiers

Magazine published by the International Oceanographic Foundation and the Rosenstiel School from 1953-1966 for those interested in the sea, from scientists to the general public.
