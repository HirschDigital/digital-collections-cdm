Manuscripts and Unpublished Works in the Libraries and Archives

About this Collection

The handwritten, drawn, painted, and unpublished works in this collection provide unique examples of artists’ works. Spanning the 18th through the 21st centuries, these singular works include friendship and autograph albums, scrapbooks, artists’ sketchbooks, archival works, and elaborate folded paper creations produced by early kindergarten teachers. Their creators range from artists trained in the fine arts to folk artists.
