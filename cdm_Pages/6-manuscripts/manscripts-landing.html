About this Collection

Singular works that span the 18th through 21st centuries, comprised of friendship albums, scrapbooks, and artists’ sketchbooks.

Collection Highlights

Friendship Albums

Albums featuring notes, drawings, and autographs from friends of the compiler.

Sketchbooks

A selection of volumes containing sketches of artists and other figures.

Early Kindergarten Teachers' Manuals

Albums of folded and woven paper and other craftwork created by kindergarten teachers as part of their education in the principles of Friedrich Fröbel.
