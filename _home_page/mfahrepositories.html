MFAH Repositories

Hirsch Library

A comprehensive art research library that serves Museum staff, external scholars, college and university students, and the general public with materials that date from the 15th century to the present

Powell Library

The Kitty King Powell Library and Study Center at Bayou Bend Collection and Gardens is one of the leading centers in the nation for resources about early American art and decorative arts of the 17th through 19th century

MFAH Archives

Established in 1984, the MFAH Archives preserves and makes available for research the Museum's permanent records

William J. Hill Texas Artisans and Artists Archive

Named for Houston collector and longtime Bayou Bend supporter William J. Hill, a freely-searchable online archive documenting the lives, work, and products of Texas artisans and artists through 1900

International Center for the Arts of the Americas

Research arm of the Latin American art department at the MFAH collecting, exhibiting, researching, and educating about the diverse artistic production of Latin American and Latinx communities globally
